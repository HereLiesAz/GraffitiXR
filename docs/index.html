Core Features

Built for the streets, not the studio. Completely local, zero cloud required.

AR Projection

Drop your sketches onto physical walls. ARCore keeps your reference anchored even when you put your phone in your pocket.

Multi-Layer Stencils

Automatically extract 1-3 layer physical stencils from any image. Export as a tiled PDF ready for standard US Letter printing.

Instant Mockups

Snap a photo of your wall and use quick 2D layering and blending tools to visualize your piece before you shake a can.

Trace (Lightbox)

Turns your phone into a high-brightness lightbox. Locks touch inputs so you can trace your screen directly onto paper.

Under the Hood: MobileGS

GraffitiXR isn't just a simple image overlay. It runs a custom C++17 native engine called MobileGS, featuring:

  • ✓ 3D Gaussian Splatting: Confidence-based voxel mapping.
  • ✓ Teleological Correction: Automatic map-to-world alignment using OpenCV fingerprinting.
  • ✓ Multi-Lens Stereo: Automatically leverages dual-camera depth on supported devices, falling back to optical flow.
  • ✓ Zero-Latency OpenGL ES 3.0: Hardware-accelerated rendering directly through the JNI bridge.
